Privacy Policy

🪔 DIVYA DISHA · दिव्य दिशा — YOUR DAILY DEVOTIONAL COMPANION
LAST UPDATED: APRIL 11, 2026

Divya Disha does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

None. Divya Disha does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, crash reports, or device identifiers. The app operates entirely offline and makes no network requests.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

Divya Disha saves the following data locally on your device to provide the devotional experience:

  • Your Dosha quiz results and Ayurvedic profile
  • Mala (japa) counting history and session records
  • Puja checklists and completion status
  • Sandhya vandana progress and schedules
  • Dinacharya (daily routine) preferences
  • Shloka bookmarks and reading progress
  • App preferences (theme, language, notification settings)

This data is stored using on-device SQLite database and local storage, and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, third party, or external service.

3. Internet Access

Divya Disha does not require or use an internet connection. The app is fully functional in airplane mode. No data is sent or received over the network.

4. Notifications

If you enable reminders for Sandhya vandana, Puja timings, or other devotional activities, Divya Disha schedules local notifications on your device using your operating system's notification service. These notifications are generated entirely on-device — no notification content, scheduling data, or delivery information is sent to any server. You can disable notifications at any time from your device's system settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Divya Disha does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting tools
  • No social media SDKs
  • No tracking or attribution services

6. Advertising

Divya Disha contains no advertisements of any kind.

7. In-App Purchases

Divya Disha contains no in-app purchases. All features are available for free.

8. Children's Privacy

Divya Disha does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar regulations by design, as no personal information is collected.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data through your device's system settings
  • Uninstalling the app (all local data is removed)

10. Permissions

Divya Disha requests the following permissions only when needed:

  • Notifications — to schedule local reminders for Sandhya, Puja, and other devotional activities. Optional and revocable.

Divya Disha does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since the app collects no data, significant changes are unlikely.
